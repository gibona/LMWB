About Shelly

Shelly is a line of Wi-Fi and Bluetooth smart home devices that let you control lights, appliances, sensors, and more. They are designed for home automation, energy monitoring, and convenience.

Popular Devices

  • Shelly 1 Gen4 – Wi-Fi relay for controlling lights & switches
  • Shelly Plug S Gen3 – Smart plug with power monitoring
  • Shelly Pro 4PM – Professional 4-channel smart relay
  • Shelly Motion 2 – Motion sensor with long battery life

Key Features

  • Works with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth
  • Energy usage monitoring
  • Cloud and local control options
  • Supports Alexa, Google Home, and MQTT
